i ogonek (Polish Hook) į [i<] similar for other letters with ogonek.
It is important to retain the various apostrophes and other diacritical marks.
Short prime (no unicode)[~] Indicates nasalized vowel.
reversed apostrophe ‘` Indicates aspiration of following vowel or consonant.
Center dot ·· Use dropdown; indicates long vowel.
Long prime ′['] Indicates the main stress.
short reversed prime ‵[`] Indicates the secondary stress.
